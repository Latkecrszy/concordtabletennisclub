Round Robins

Every Monday and Wednesday night, players are divided into groups of 5 or 6 by rating and play a round robin against everyone in their group. Results are posted after each session. Ratings are updated based on wins and losses within the group.

Current session results

Results for each session are posted to Google Sheets after the night ends. Monday and Wednesday results are on separate sheets.
