Membership

The club is open to everyone, members and non-members alike. Membership is $60 per year and covers all family members living at the same address. Membership is prorated by quarter.

Non-member drop-in rate: $15 per night (first visit $10, under 18 always $5).

Monthly pass (members only)

Members can also sign up for a $65/month auto-pay plan via credit card, which covers unlimited drop-in nights for that month. This is a good deal if you play more than 6 times a month.

Benefits

  • Reduced drop-in rate ($10 vs. $15)
  • Notification of club activities, programs, and tournaments
  • News related to table tennis locally and nationally
  • Membership covers your entire household

How to join

Download the membership form, fill it out, and bring it along with your membership fee on your next visit. Fees can be paid by cash, check, Zelle, or Venmo at the front desk.

Questions? Contact us at the front desk on any Monday or Wednesday night, or use the contact form.
