About the Club

The Concord Table Tennis Club has been in continuous operation since 1972. We are affiliated with USA Table Tennis (USATT), the national governing body for the sport.

The club's mission is to provide a friendly yet competitive playing space, with good lighting, quality tables, nets, and balls for everyone who wants to enjoy the sport. Beginners, novice, intermediate, and advanced players are all welcome.

We are a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization, volunteer run, and open to the public. The club is located in the Ultimate Fieldhouse in Walnut Creek and holds round robin play twice a week. Walk-ins are always welcome, and loaner paddles are available.
