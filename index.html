How a night works

Competitive round robin

12 tables are set aside for the round robin. Players are divided into groups by rating and play everyone in their group. Sign up in person between 6:45 and 7:15pm. Play starts at 7:30pm.

  • Running late? Text (925) 238-3505 before 7pm and your spot will be held.
  • Pay in advance via Zelle or Venmo and skip the front desk. Text to confirm.

Open play

3 to 5 tables are available for casual play, coaching, and doubles. Usually more open play tables on Wednesdays. Open tables follow a challenge format: winner stays for two rounds, then players rotate in.

Membership

Annual membership is $60 (prorated: $45 from April, $30 from July, $15 from October). Covers all family members at the same address. Members pay $10 per night instead of $15.

See the membership page for benefits and how to join.
